Privacy Policy — SP Audit

Effective date: 25 June 2026

SP Audit ("the App") is operated by Andrew Rader. This policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and your rights in relation to it.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects only information you provide directly:

  • Audit data — site names, addresses, issue descriptions, risk ratings, notes, and checklist responses you enter manually.
  • Photos — images you take or select from your gallery when documenting audit issues or site assessments.

The App does not collect your name, email address, location data, device identifiers, or any other personal information automatically.

2. How Your Information Is Used

All data you enter is used solely to:

  • Display and organise your audits, checklists, and site assessments within the App.
  • Generate PDF reports that you can save or share at your discretion.

3. Data Storage

All data — including photos and generated reports — is stored locally on your device only. Nothing is uploaded to any server or cloud service. We have no access to your data at any time.

4. Camera Permission

The App requests access to your device camera solely to allow you to photograph audit issues and site conditions. Photos are saved to the App's private storage on your device and are never transmitted externally.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or transfer your data to any third parties. When you use the App's share function to send a PDF report, that action is performed entirely by you using your device's standard sharing features — the App has no involvement beyond preparing the file.

6. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any analytics, advertising, or crash-reporting services. No third-party SDKs that collect data are included.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data persists on your device until you delete it within the App or uninstall the App. Uninstalling the App removes all stored data permanently.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for use by workplace professionals and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the effective date above. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
